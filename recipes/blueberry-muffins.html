Blueberry Muffins

Description

These blueberry muffins are quick and easy with a moist and tender center bursting with blueberries and perfectly golden brown top. They are extra easy to make, too. For another spin on these muffins, try our lemon blueberry muffins recipe or for egg-free and dairy-free muffins, try these vegan blueberry muffins.

Ingredients

  • fresh or frozen blueberries
  • all-purpose flour
  • granulated sugar
  • oil
  • milk
  • egg
  • baking powder

Recipe

  1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and a little salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add oil, an egg, milk, and vanilla extract then stir with a fork until blended.
  3. Mix in the blueberries, divide the batter between muffin cups and bake.
